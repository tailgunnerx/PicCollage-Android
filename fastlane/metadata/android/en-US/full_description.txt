A high-performance, native Android photo collage studio built entirely with Jetpack Compose.

Features:
- 🎨 26 Dynamic Gradient Borders: Neon, Cyberpunk, Holographic, and more.
- 📐 10 Pro-Style Layouts: From single frames to complex asymmetric grids.
- 🖼️ Slot-Based Photo Management: Add photos exactly where you want them.
- 🔄 Image Manipulation: 90° rotation and easy photo removal.
- 🌈 Advanced Color Control: Full-spectrum picker and vibrant palettes.
- 💾 High-Resolution Export: 1080x1080px crisp output saved to your Gallery.

Optimized for speed and aesthetic control with zero external library overhead. 100% Offline and Private.
